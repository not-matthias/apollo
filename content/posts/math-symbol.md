# Inline Math

- $(a+b)^2$ = $a^2 + 2ab + b^2$
- A polynomial P of degree d over $\mathbb{F}_p$ is an expression of the form
  $P(s) = a_0 + a_1 . s + a_2 . s^2 + ... + a_d . s^d$ for some
  $a_0,..,a_d \in \mathbb{F}_p$
